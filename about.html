ABOUT US

Sumer Restaurant Group, with an Arabic and Iraqi flavor, distinguished dishes The first specialized restaurant that mixes popular and western food in the Middle East. Our experience exceeds 60 years of experience and excellence in providing delicious food. Our beginning was in 1949 in the city of Sumer, where we were and still are without competition. From others it is to provide new and varied dishes in order to satisfy all tastes. We are distinguished from others in the authentic oriental taste of seafood We also excel in offering shrimp, squid, fillet and liver sandwiches, and we challenge those who compete with us if there is someone who can compete with us. One of the most important things that distinguishes us also is that we offer all the arts of cooking fish, casseroles and trays, and seasoning fries and grills with our own seasoning, and no one can offer what we offer because we have the secret of craftsmanship We also have specialists in the world of salads. We have tomato salad, our own tahini salad, eggplant roasted with garlic, pickled cucumbers with the magic mixture, papaganog salad, and refreshing yogurt salad. We also offer three different services to satisfy all customers. We have a very distinguished 5-star dining hall, and we have distinguished workers in serving food
